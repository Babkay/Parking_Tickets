Vancouver Parking Rate Analysis

This interactive choropleth map reveals parking meter rate variations across Vancouver neighborhoods, addressing two critical questions:

1. Which areas have the highest and lowest parking rates?
The color-coded design instantly highlights pricing extremes, from affordable zones to premium areas.

2. Where do pricing clusters emerge?
Geographic patterns become clear, showing whether high-rate meters concentrate in commercial districts or tourist hotspots.

Hover over neighborhoods to view exact rates, with darker shades indicating higher prices. The legend provides quick reference for rate ranges across the city.
