User Instructions

  1. Select an Area: Use the dropdown to filter data.
  2. X-Axis: Displays day and time intervals.
  3. Y-Axis: Average parking rate in dollars.
  4. Hover: View detailed values and highlight the selected line.
  5. Legend: Shows color codes for each area (when all areas are shown).

This interactive line chart shows how average hourly parking meter rates in Vancouver vary across different times of day and days of the week. It helps answer two key questions:

  • 1. How do average parking rates change throughout the week?
    Identify if rates are higher on weekdays vs weekends, or during specific time slots.
  • 2. How do rates compare between local areas?
    Compare locations like Downtown, Mount Pleasant, and Kitsilano to see rate differences.

Use the dropdown to explore individual areas or view all areas together. Hover over the lines to explore detailed rate data.
